As a curious and imaginative young person, I have always been drawn to art. Pursuing a bachelor's degree in architecture allowed me to channel my artistic interests in a more structured way, while still maintaining the raw and authentic approach that is evident in my work.

My goal as an architect is to inspire others through my work and provide a respite from the fast-paced routine of everyday life. My projects often feature a vibrant use of color, expressive mark making, and interesting characters that come together to create a unique visual language.

I am guided by my deep intuition and connection to art, and I find great joy in the process of creation. By allowing myself to lose myself completely in the work and following a rhythmic pattern, I am able to witness the pieces come to life and reveal their direction. Through naive exploration, my anxious and lost characters take on a life of their own, expressing their internal worlds through rhythmic patterns, shapes, and symbologies that bind the work together.

I am eager to continue on this never-ending journey and work in a profession that combines my love of art and architecture.
