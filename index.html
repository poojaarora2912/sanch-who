I, Sanchi Mahavirchand, am a curious young person stumbling to tame my artsy highly imaginative mind. Art drew me in at a very young age and ever since , it’s always been me yearning to draw outside the lines.

Pursuing architecture for my bachelor’s degree opened up the possibility to streamline and enhance my approach to art whilst maintaining the raw and honest authentic approach that has consistently prevailed in my work.

An escape from the fast paced routine life is what I yearn to provide by inspiring people through art and architecture. My work includes a huge explosion of colour; a good mix of expressive mark making and interesting characters that translate to a distinct visual language.

Letting my deep intuitive bond with art guide me, harmoniously following a rhythmic work pattern and losing myself completely in the humble journey of creation allows me to witness the pieces magically reveal themselves and their direction. Through naive exploration, Life is blown into the souls of my anxious lost characters expressing their own internal world illustrated in the form of rhythmic patterns , shapes and symbologies cohesively binding the work together.

I'm eager to immerse myself in this never-ending kind of life and work in a profession that unites art and architecture.
